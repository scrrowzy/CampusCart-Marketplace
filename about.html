About Campus Cart

A Safe Student Marketplace Within the Campus

Our Story

Many students struggle to sell or purchase used school-related items in a safe and reliable way. Traditional social media marketplaces often lack proper moderation, identity verification, and organization, leading to scams and unsafe transactions.

Campus Cart was created to provide a secure, campus-exclusive marketplace where students can confidently buy and sell items within their school community.

Our goal is to make buying and selling easier, safer, and more organized for every student.
